WHO WE ARE

At Olachea Group, we are a multi-sector company rooted in agriculture and expanded into the primary, chemical, and industrial markets. With over 84 years working the land, our legacy began in the fields and continues to grow worldwide.

Born from a family of farmers, our journey has been one of transformation — blending tradition with precision agriculture and cutting-edge technology to create sustainable, resource-efficient solutions for modern farming.

Empowering Agriculture, Feeding the Future

At Olachea Group, our mission is to strengthen national and global food security through agricultural logistics, financing, and high-quality solutions that meet the evolving needs of farmers and agri-businesses worldwide

Delivering innovative tools and technologies for the field
Driving practical development that accelerates agribusiness growth
Building solid, ethical partnerships across the supply chain

Cultivating the Next Generation of Agriculture

Our vision is to become a recognized global leader in agronomic innovation, connecting the primary sector to advanced industrial operations with seamless, tech-driven efficiency.

Automation software for connected, intelligent farming
