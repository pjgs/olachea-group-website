Three Tiers of Excellence: Our Export Portfolio

Our production is segmented into three specific categories designed to fit different market needs, ensuring that whether you are a boutique roaster or a large-scale distributor, we have the right bean for you.

1. Fresh Green Coffee (Café Verde)

The Canvas for Master Roasters

Our Green Coffee is the raw expression of Venezuelan terroir. Sourced from the best harvest windows, this coffee is processed to preserve its intrinsic enzymatic integrity. It is ideal for roasters who want full control over the flavor development profile.

  • • Process: Natural or Washed options available
  • • Profile: High density, vibrant acidity potential, excellent shelf stability
  • • Best For: Roasters creating signature house blends or experimenting with roast profiles
Download Technical Sheet

2. Specialty Coffee – 100% Arabic (Café Especialidad)

The Jewel of the Harvest

This is our flagship product: 100% Arabic Coffee. Grown at high altitudes (typically above 1,200 MASL), these beans are selectively hand-picked at peak ripeness. This category represents the pinnacle of Venezuelan coffee agriculture.

  • • Variety: Typica, Bourbon, and Caturra
  • • Cupping Score: 84+ points (SCA)
  • • Flavor Notes: Complex profiles featuring citrus zest, caramel, and floral hints
  • • Best For: Third-wave coffee shops and premium single-origin retail lines
Download Technical Sheet

3. Grade 2 Washed Coffee (Café Lavado)

Consistency and Balance

Our Grade 2 Washed Coffee offers the perfect balance between quality and volume. It undergoes a rigorous washing and fermentation process that ensures a clean cup with a smooth finish. It is the workhorse of our export line, delivering reliability in every sack.

  • • Process: Fully washed and sun-dried
  • • Profile: Clean body, low defects, and balanced acidity
  • • Best For: Commercial blends, espresso bases, and high-volume distribution
